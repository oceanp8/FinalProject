Rules:







1. Get to the very top by jumping through the platforms and solving each question







2. You have 3 lives and if you get questions wrong 3 times, the game restarts







3. Questions are randomized per topic







4. Math topics start with algebra into geometry, statistics, trigonometry and ends with calculus







5. Science topics start with biology, earth science, environmental science, chemistry, and ends with physics







6. Controls start at UP, LEFT, RIGHT arrow but can be changed







7. ENJOY!!!
